Ditch the SIM card
Wanderr uses eSIM cards, digital SIM cards that can connect your phone or device to a mobile network without needing any physical SIM card.
Hassle free
Finding and purchasing a local SIM card is a hassle - some require significant personal information, or won't even give visitors SIM cards. Our digital marketplace lets you find and purchase your local SIM card all from your own phone.
Get connected immediately
How much time do you spend trying to connect to the airport WiFi upon landing in a new country? With Wanderr, set up your SIM card plan before you leave and get connected immedately upon arrival at your international destination.
Skip the roaming fees
How much does your current carrier charge for data roaming? $10/day? More? Wanderr plans are as cheap as $3/week for 1GB of data.
Keep your original phone plan active
Revolutionary eSIM technology allows you to keep multiple phone plans active at once, meaning you can receive calls and texts on your primary number while using a Wanderr plan for global data connectivity.
Plans for over 100 countries
We offer data plans for over 100 countries and regions. Wherever you're going, we've got you covered.

Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

What countries does Wanderr support?

We will soon support data plans for over 100 countries, including most popular destinations within Europe, Asia, South America, and more.

How are plans priced?

We offer prepaid eSIM cards that are active for a set number of days and for a set amount of data. You can also top up additional data to your prepaid card.

What are the payment methods you accept?

We're currently accepting payments via PayPal.

When will you be adding support for this new country I'm interested in traveling to?

We are constantly adding new data plans to our marketplace! Please contact us if you wish to express interest in any seeing data plans for any specific countries or regions.

What is your refund policy?

Unfortunately, because your eSIM card becomes active as soon as you purchase it, we are unable to offer refunds at this time.

How can I contact you?

Please reach out to our team at support@trywanderr.com.
